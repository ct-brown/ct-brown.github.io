## Why "Ecosocial"?

Climate risk is never "just" environmentalâ€”it's fundamentally social. Effective adaptation requires integrated systems of **productive infrastructure and care**: cooling centers and flood protection *alongside* income support, housing, healthcare, and worker protections.

**Key Principles:**

- **Integration**: Linking climate adaptation with social protection systems
- **Justice**: Centering those most affected by climate impacts in policy design
- **Transformation**: Moving beyond market-based solutions to address root causes
- **Community Leadership**: Building power through education and organizing
- **Whole-System Response**: Addressing ecological and social dimensions together
