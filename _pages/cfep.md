## Why "Ecosocial"?

Climate risk is never "just" environmental‚Äîit's social, political, and economic. Effective adaptation requires integrated systems of **infrastructure and care**: cooling centers and flood protection alongside housing security, income support, healthcare access, and worker protections.

**Ecosocial adaptation means meeting human needs while transforming the systems that produce vulnerability in the first place.**
